About
The Oven Restaurants are proudly owned/operated by a dedicated family of chefs who have been creating Indian cuisine for over twenty seven years. The Oven Restaurants specialize in Northern Indian Cuisine which is known for its rich colors, flavor profiles and unique style of cooking. Whether you enjoy our original Haymarket location, founded in 1988, complemented by the relaxed vibe of The Cellar, the beautiful sunsets and outdoor patio of The Oven East, we welcome you to join us, and enjoy the culture and cuisine of India.
